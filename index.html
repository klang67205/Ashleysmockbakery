Meet Ashley's Story

From Teacher to Baker

Ashley Martinez spent 12 years as an elementary school teacher before discovering her true calling in the kitchen. After watching countless fundraising efforts struggle with low-quality products, she decided to create something different.

"I knew educators deserved better," Ashley says. "Both in the appreciation they receive and the tools they have for fundraising success."

Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 How far in advance should I order?

For best results, we recommend 2-3 weeks notice. This allows time for custom design, ingredient sourcing, and stress-free planning. Rush orders (48-hour minimum) are available for a 25% fee.

💰 What are typical profit margins?

Our fundraising packages offer 65-75% profit margins. For example, items that sell for $4 cost your organization $1-1.40. We provide detailed profit projections during consultation.

🎨 Can you match our school colors exactly?

Yes! Send us your school colors, logo, or mascot and we'll create digital mockups for approval. We use professional food coloring to match even the most specific shades.

📦 How are items packaged?

Every item comes individually wrapped in eco-friendly packaging with custom labels featuring your school information. Perfect for grab-and-go sales or gift giving.
